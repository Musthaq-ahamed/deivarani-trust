About Us

DEIVA RANI EDUCATIONAL AND HEALTHCARE CHARITABLE TRUST was founded in 2022 by Dr. Deeban Chakkaravarthy M.P.T(Paed), Ph.D with the aim of rehabilitation of Neuro-development children and Geriatric people with great facility and accessibility.

Our trust is committed to providing comprehensive care, support, and rehabilitation services to improve the quality of life for those we serve.

Our Mission
